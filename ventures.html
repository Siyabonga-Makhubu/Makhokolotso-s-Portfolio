Single Moms with Purpose

Single Moms with Purpose is an organization dedicated to empowering, inspiring, and supporting single mothers as they navigate life's journey with confidence, resilience, and purpose. We believe that every mother has unique gifts, strengths, and potential that can positively impact her family, community, and future. Our organization creates opportunities for personal growth, leadership development, healing, mentorship, networking, and community support, helping single mothers overcome challenges, pursue their goals, and build meaningful lives for themselves and their children. At Single Moms with Purpose, we are committed to helping women move beyond survival and embrace a life of purpose, impact, and fulfillment. Slogan: Fulfilling God's Mandate

Faith-Inspired Clothing Brand

Hey Queen

Hey Queen was inspired by the belief that every woman carries strength, purpose, and greatness within her, regardless of the challenges she faces. Founded by Makhokolotso Khapetsi, the brand was born from a personal journey of overcoming adversity, choosing faith over fear, and refusing to be defined by circumstances. Hey Queen is more than a clothing brand—it is a movement that empowers women to embrace their worth, walk confidently in their purpose, and keep believing in themselves. Every design is created to inspire resilience, courage, self-love, and faith, serving as a daily reminder that no setback is greater than the strength within. Our message is simple: You are strong. You are capable. You are chosen. You are a Queen. Wear your power. Own your story.
