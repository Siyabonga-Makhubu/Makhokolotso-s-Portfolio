About

Driven by faith and a passion for inspiring others, she has dedicated her life to creating platforms that encourage personal growth, healing, and purpose.

Through her writing, speaking engagements, businesses, and initiatives, she continues to inspire others to embrace their purpose, trust God's plan, and pursue their dreams with courage and determination.

By the end of 2020, I hosted my first "Greet and Meet" event for single mothers, where women came together to connect, share experiences, encourage one another, and heal together. That event reminded me how powerful it is when women support each other.

As my work continued growing, I also founded a faith-inspired clothing brand called Hey Queen, created to encourage women to embrace their identity, confidence, and purpose.

From 2020 until today, I have continued speaking at public events, churches, podcasts, community gatherings, radio stations, and online platforms. Every opportunity allows me to touch lives and inspire meaningful change.

In 2025, I became a published author alongside other women when we co-authored Her Story, Her Journey. Through that experience, we formed a movement called Authors on the Move, a group of women committed to using writing as a tool for healing, storytelling, and empowerment.

In May 2026, I published my own journal titled My Prayer Journal, a faith-based journal created to help people reflect, pray, heal, and reconnect with themselves spiritually. Writing became another extension of my voice and purpose.

I do not live my life only for myself. I am deeply aware of what is happening around me—the pain people carry, the struggles women face, the confusion many young people experience, and the need for healing in communities. Because of that awareness, I continue evolving, learning, and growing so that I can better serve others.

I strongly believe in self-development and research. I constantly read, learn, observe, and expand my knowledge so that I can become more effective in the work I do. My journey is still continuing, and I believe my story is only the beginning of something greater.
